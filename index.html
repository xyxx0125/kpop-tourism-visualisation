K-pop Popularity and Tourism to South Korea

Hypothesis

Countries with higher levels of K-pop attention tend to send more visitors to South Korea per 100,000 population.

Methodology

This study combines international tourism arrivals with a K-pop attention index derived from digital cultural signals. Tourism figures are normalised by national population to allow meaningful cross-country comparison.
